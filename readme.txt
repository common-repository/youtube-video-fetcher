==Description==
Are you a blogger, who is searching for videos and then posting it on your site? Then relax and have a look at this plugin,
Youtube Video Fetcher is a wordpress plugin which is designed to fetch the videos from youtube and display it on your wordpress site.
It fetches the title of the post and then it searches on the youtube, after this it fecthes the youtube video and displays it on the site after the post.

= Options =
The admin has the options menu in the wp-admin section, where admin can set the number of videos to be shown after the post.
Navigate to the settings->Youtube Video Settings and change the settings.

Now you can also hide videos for some posts, just go into post and uncheck the show video checkbox(by deafult it is checked).

==Installation==
1. Download
2. Unzip the package and upload to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
3. Log into WordPress and navigate to the "Plugins" panel.
4. Activate the plugin.
